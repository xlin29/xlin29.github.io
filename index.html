I am a Computer Science Ph.D. candidate advised by Prof. Jason Polakis at University of Illinois Chicago. My research interests revolve around security and privacy. My objectives are to investigate the Web's threat landscape and develop countermeasures to protect user privacy. Lately, I've been focusing on browser fingerprinting techniques and tackling pitfalls in authentication/authorisation mechanisms. I received my M.Sc. from Northeastern University and B.Sc. from Xiamen University.
